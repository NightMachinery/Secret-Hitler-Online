# Policy Card Claims

After a successful legislative policy is enacted, the game pauses before any presidential power resolves. The president and chancellor must each either report which policy cards they say they saw or choose **Refuse to tell**.

- The president reports three cards.
- The chancellor reports two cards.
- Claims are table talk aids only: the server records what the player claims, not whether it matches the hidden hand.
- In games with Anarchist policies enabled, claims can include Anarchist cards. Standard games only offer Liberal and Fascist claim cards.
- Bot-controlled seats automatically refuse so the game does not stall or reveal perfect bot information.

Lobby creators can enable **Show policy card claims** in history settings. When enabled, the history panel shows past-round claims with tiny policy cards. When disabled, past claims are hidden, but the current round's claims remain visible while that round is active so players can discuss them.
